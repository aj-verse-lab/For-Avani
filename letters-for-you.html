When I miss you
I miss you in the quietest way.

Not the kind that hurts, but the kind that lingers. The kind that makes me imagine you reading this and smiling softly. The kind that reminds me how naturally you have become a part of my days. Distance only makes me more sure of you.
What I notice about you
I notice how you make spaces warmer just by being in them. How people relax around you. How you listen with your whole heart. You do not try to be special. You just are. And I fall a little more every time I notice that.
What I feel when you choose me
When you choose me, even in small ways, it makes me want to protect that choice. To be gentle with you. To be steady. To make sure you always feel safe where you stand with me.
What I imagine when I think of us
I imagine calm mornings. Unnecessary laughter. Long conversations that do not need conclusions. Your head resting comfortably. My hand finding yours without thinking. A life that grows slowly, but deeply.
What I want you to remember tonight
You are not alone. You do not have to carry everything by yourself. You are allowed to rest. And somewhere, there is someone who is very glad you exist. That someone is me.
The one thing I have not said enough
Loving you does not feel dramatic. It feels certain. It feels calm. And it makes me want to meet you again, just to sit next to you and smile for no reason.
